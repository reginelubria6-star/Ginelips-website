Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is GineSlips?
GineSlips is a brand focused on providing comfortable, stylish, and high-quality everyday essentials designed to fit your lifestyle.

2. What products do you offer?
We offer carefully designed items that combine comfort, durability, and modern style. Check our shop page to see our latest collections.

3. How do I choose the right size?
We provide a detailed size guide on each product page to help you find your perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for extra comfort.

4. Do you ship nationwide?
Yes, we offer nationwide shipping. Delivery times may vary depending on your location.

5. How long does shipping take?
Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days after shipping.

6. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment options, including online payments and cash on delivery (if available in your area).

7. Can I return or exchange my order?
Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within a limited period after delivery, as long as the item is unused and in its original condition.

8. How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number so you can monitor its progress.

9. What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?
Please contact us immediately with photos of the item, and we’ll make it right as quickly as possible.

10. How can I contact GineSlips?
You can reach us through our contact page or email us directly. We’re happy to assist you with any questions or concerns.
